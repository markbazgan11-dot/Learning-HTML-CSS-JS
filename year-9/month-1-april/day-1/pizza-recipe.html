Pepperoni Pizza

Crispy, cheesy, and perfectly spicy. Discover the ultimate homemade pepperoni pizza recipe.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pepperoni Slices
  • Dried Oregano

Instructions

  1. Prep: Preheat your oven to 450°F–475°F (230–245°C). Place a baking sheet or pizza stone inside while it heats.
  2. Shape: On a floured surface, roll or stretch your dough into a 12-inch circle. Transfer to a parchment-lined pan.
  3. Assemble: Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce, then top with mozzarella and pepperoni.
  4. Season: Sprinkle with dried oregano. Optionally brush the crust edge with olive oil.
  5. Bake: Bake for 10–15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.

Enjoy Your Pizza!
