Star Wars: Revenge of the Sith

Star Wars: Episode III – Revenge of the Sith (2005) follows Anakin Skywalker's tragic fall to the dark side, seduced by Palpatine's promise of power to save Padmé. As the Clone Wars end, the Jedi are decimated by Order 66, resulting in the rise of the Galactic Empire and the birth of Darth Vader. The film concludes with the twins Luke and Leia hidden, setting the stage for the original trilogy.
